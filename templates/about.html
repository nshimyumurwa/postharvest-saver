About PostHarvestSaver

🎯 The Problem

Rwanda loses an estimated 30% of its agricultural produce after harvest every season. This isn't a farming failure — it's an information gap. Smallholder farmers lack access to real-time weather-aware storage guidance tailored to their specific crops and locations.

💡 Our Solution

PostHarvestSaver combines live weather data from OpenWeatherMap with crop-specific spoilage science to calculate real-time risk scores for farmers across all 30 Rwandan districts.

🇷🇼 Rwanda Impact

This project directly supports Rwanda's Vision 2050 and NST2 goals for agricultural transformation and food security.

🔌 APIs Used

OpenWeatherMap Current Weather API — Provides real-time temperature, humidity, wind speed, and rainfall data.
